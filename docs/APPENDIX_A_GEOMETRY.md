# Part I Appendix A – Love · Truth · Power Geometry

This appendix provides a more detailed view of the Love/Truth/Power geometry referenced in Chapter 2.

- The decision space is represented as a circle with an equilateral triangle inside.
- The vertices correspond to Love, Truth, and Power.
- The circle is divided into six sectors, each representing a primary pairing:
  - Love–Truth, Truth–Love
  - Truth–Power, Power–Truth
  - Love–Power, Power–Love

For each serious proposal, the family records:

- Primary axis (Love, Truth, or Power).
- Sector (which pair is in focus).
- Ring (family/core vs civilization/context).

This geometry is a routing grammar for governance, not only a metaphor.
