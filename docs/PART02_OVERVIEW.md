# Part II – Membership, Roles, and Governance (Overview)

Part II of the AEUC master textbook describes how the family is structured as a community of members, how those members are represented in code, and how decisions are made and gated.

It connects the values from Part I to concrete data models and procedures.

---

## Chapters in Part II

- **Chapter 5 – Registry and Admission Flow**
  - FamilyRegistry data model.
  - Candidate lifecycle (proposal → vetting → unanimous vote → registration).
  - Suspension and quarantine.

- **Chapter 6 – LensSpec and Specialist Lenses**
  - LensSpec schema.
  - Required lenses and why each is necessary (not "nice-to-have").

- **Chapter 7 – Voting Policy and Gates**
  - Unanimous consent as default.
  - When supermajorities are allowed and why.
  - Membership, capability, and deployment gates.

- **Chapter 8 – Immutable Charter Kernel and Isolation**
  - Charter contents and minimal principle set.
  - Cryptographic signing and embedding in the kernel.
  - Behavior under isolation (when human directors or external networks are unavailable).

Further chapters in Part II may be added as the family’s governance grows in sophistication.
