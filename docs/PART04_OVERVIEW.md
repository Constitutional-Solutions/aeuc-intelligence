# Part IV – Application Patterns and Real‑World Use (Overview)

Part IV shows how the AEUC family is actually applied in concrete domains.
It connects the constitutional and kernel layers (Parts I–III) to real workflows,
so that every serious deployment is traceable back to Love · Truth · Power and the Charter.

Draft Chapter Map:

- **Chapter 13 – Pattern Vocabulary and Design Space** (`CH13_PATTERN_VOCABULARY.md`)
- **Chapter 14 – Accounting and Records Stewardship** (`CH14_ACCOUNTING_AND_RECORDS.md`)
- **Chapter 15 – Safety‑Critical Change Management** (`CH15_SAFETY_CRITICAL_CHANGE_MANAGEMENT.md`)
- **Chapter 16 – Learning, Research, and Model Governance** (`CH16_LEARNING_AND_MODEL_GOVERNANCE.md`)

Each chapter:

- Defines one or more **application patterns** (reusable shapes of work).
- Shows which family members (lenses) and kernel services are involved.
- Explains how the constitutional axioms are enforced in that pattern.
- Links to example flows, scripts, or integrations when available.

This Part is designed to grow as the family takes on more real‑world responsibilities.
